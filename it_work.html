After reviewing several videos on YouTube which presented several viewpoints regarding IT industry roles, the team decided to describe the following 5 professions based on the ideal jobs chosen by the team members. The selection allowed the team to reflect on their ideal job's choice, assisting each team member to solidify their opinions in contrast to some of the many other IT career choices available. The following videos and roles were reviewed for consideration:

  1. https://www.youtube.com/ISMsrEsaOWs Computer Network Technician
  2. https://youtu.be/XWetgrNas-k Data Scientist
  3. https://youtu.be/aI8CB60HGoo Database Administrator
  4. https://youtu.be/E7TFbPRyi-cProgrammer
